Facts

Here are some facts about the universe:

  • The universe is estimated to be around 13.8 billion years old.
  • The universe is made up of approximately 100 billion galaxies.
  • The universe is expanding at a rate of approximately 0.5% per year.
  • The universe contains a massive amount of dark energy.
  • The universe is composed of a mix of matter and energy.
